Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Introduction

Rhodes AGI LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Rhodes AGI application, TotalControl application, and website (rhodesagi.com). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across all our products.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and password. Email is optional.

Conversation Data: We store your conversations with Rhodes to enable session history and continuity. Conversations are processed by third-party AI providers (see Data Sharing).

Usage Data: We collect basic analytics about app usage to improve our service.

Mobile App Permissions

Our mobile apps may request access to the following device features. All permissions are optional and requested only when needed:

  • Camera: To take photos for analysis by the AI assistant. Photos are sent to our server for processing and are not stored permanently.
  • Microphone: To enable voice chat with the AI assistant. Audio is processed in real-time and is not permanently recorded.
  • Photo Library: To share images from your library with the AI assistant.
  • Location: Only when explicitly requested by you through the AI assistant. Location data is not tracked or stored.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: For secure authentication. Biometric data never leaves your device and is handled entirely by the iOS operating system.
  • Notifications: To deliver alerts from the AI assistant.
  • Files: To access documents you choose to share with the AI assistant via the document picker.

TotalControl App

Rhodes Total Control is a self-control and website blocking app. Here is how it handles your data:

DNS Filtering: When you enable DNS filtering, your device's DNS queries are routed through our DNS-over-HTTPS server. This means our server processes the domain names you visit to apply your blocking rules. We do not log or permanently store your DNS queries. DNS query data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

HealthKit & Health Data: TotalControl reads workout data (workout type, duration, distance, calories) from Apple HealthKit to evaluate fitness-based blocking conditions. Workout data is transmitted to our server to log workouts and check status. This data is used solely to evaluate your blocking rules and is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or stored in iCloud.

Step Counting: TotalControl uses CoreMotion to count daily steps for step-based blocking conditions. Step data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Location Data: TotalControl uses location services for GPS run tracking and gym geofencing. Geofence data (gym name, coordinates, radius) is stored on our server. GPS route data during runs is processed on-device. You can revoke location access in Settings.

Device Information: We collect your device name and a vendor identifier to associate your device with your account for DNS filtering. A heartbeat signal is sent periodically containing device identifier, app version, and DNS status.

Speech & Microphone: The microphone and Apple Speech Recognition are used for voice chat with the AI Advisor. Audio is processed on-device by Apple's speech recognition. Only the transcribed text is sent to our chat API.

Blocking Rules: Your blocking rules are stored on our server to sync across features and are deleted when you delete your account.

AI Advisor Chat: Messages sent to the AI Advisor are processed by our AI infrastructure. Chat history is stored locally on your device.

Screen Analysis: The in-app browser may analyze on-screen content using Apple's Vision framework for feed detection. This occurs entirely on-device.

AI Processing: The AI Advisor feature sends your chat messages to Rhodes, an AI by Rhodes AGI LLC. Messages are processed to generate responses and execute blocking rule changes. We do not use your chat data to train AI models. Chat messages are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Account Deletion: You can delete your account from the Settings tab. This removes all your data from our servers.

Google Drive Integration

Rhodes can connect to your Google Drive account to help you work with documents. When you enable this integration:

  • Read Access: Rhodes can read files in your Google Drive that you choose to share with the assistant, to provide context-aware help.
  • File Creation: Rhodes can create and edit new files in your Google Drive on your behalf (e.g., drafting documents, saving summaries).
  • No Deletion: Rhodes cannot delete your existing Google Drive files.
  • Revocable: You can disconnect Google Drive access at any time through your Google Account permissions at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Google Drive data accessed by Rhodes is processed by the same AI providers listed in our Data Sharing section. We do not store copies of your Drive files on our servers beyond the duration of your conversation.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Rhodes AGI and TotalControl services
  • To enable conversation history and session persistence
  • To improve and optimize our services
  • To communicate with you about your account

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • AI service providers: Conversations are processed by Rhodes, an AI by Rhodes AGI LLC. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Service providers: Who assist in operating our infrastructure
  • Law enforcement: When required by law

TotalControl data (browsing history, blocked apps, OCR results) is never sent to our servers or any third party.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communications are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS). Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain.

Data Retention

We retain your conversation history until you delete your account. Guest sessions are retained for 30 days. Local app data (TotalControl settings, bookmarks, history) persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your conversation history through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Revoke any device permission at any time through iOS Settings
  • Clear local browsing data in TotalControl at any time
  • Contact us with privacy questions

Children's Privacy

Rhodes AGI and TotalControl are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
