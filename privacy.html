Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Introduction

Rhodes AI LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Rhodes AI application and website (rhodesagi.com). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and username.

Conversation Data: We store your conversations with Rhodes to enable session history and continuity.

Usage Data: We collect basic analytics about app usage to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Rhodes AI service
  • To enable conversation history and session persistence
  • To improve and optimize our service
  • To communicate with you about your account

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • AI service providers to process your requests (conversations are sent to AI APIs)
  • Service providers who assist in operating our service
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Conversations are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS).

Data Retention

We retain your conversation history until you delete your account. Guest sessions are retained for 30 days.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your conversation history through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Contact us with privacy questions

Children's Privacy

Rhodes AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes.
