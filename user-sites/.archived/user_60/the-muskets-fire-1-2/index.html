THE MUSKETS FIRE

PLAYER 1 (Top Screen)

WASD - Move

Mouse - Aim

Click - Fire / Cock

R - Start Reload Sequence

Hold R - Check Pan (see if loaded)

F - Iron Sights

PLAYER 2 (Bottom Screen)

Gamepad Left Stick - Move

Gamepad Right Stick - Aim

RT (R2) - Fire / Cock

RB (R1) - Start Reload

Hold RB - Check Pan

LT (L2) - Iron Sights

NO HUD. Check your weapon manually. Watch for smoke. Mind the drop.

PAUSED - Click to Resume
