Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a selection of gluten-free baked goods available daily. Please note we bake in a kitchen that also handles gluten, so we cannot guarantee no cross-contamination.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend at least 48 hours notice for custom cakes. For weddings or large events, please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance.

Do you deliver?

We offer local delivery within 5 miles for orders over $25. Delivery fees start at $5.

Can I place a large order for an event?

Absolutely! We cater events of all sizes. Please contact us at least one week in advance for large orders.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have a small parking lot behind the bakery and street parking is available on Baker Street.

Are you hiring?

We're always looking for passionate bakers and friendly staff! Please stop by with your resume or email us.
