MUSKETS FIRE

2-Player Split-Screen Musket Duel

PLAYER 1 (Top Screen)

Move: WASD

Aim: Mouse

Iron Sights: Q

Fire: F

Reload: R

PLAYER 2 (Bottom Screen)

Move: IJKL (I=forward, J=left, K=back, L=right)

Aim: Arrow Keys

Iron Sights: O

Fire: Semicolon (;)

Reload: P

No HUD: Check your weapon to see if it's loaded!

Reload: Pour powder, patch, ball, ramrod, prime, cock. ~7 seconds.

Iron Sights: Line up front and rear sights for accuracy.
