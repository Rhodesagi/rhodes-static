THE MUSKET DUEL

A Historical Flintlock Duel

PLAYER 1 (Top)

Mouse - Aim

WASD - Move

LMB / F - Fire

R - Advance Reload Step

PLAYER 2 (Bottom)

Arrow Keys - Aim

IJKL - Move

ENTER / ; - Fire

P - Advance Reload Step

Iron sights only. No crosshairs. Reloading requires 6 precise steps.
