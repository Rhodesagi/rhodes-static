MUSKETEER DUEL

Two players. One computer. Flintlock muskets.

Aim with iron sights only. No crosshairs.

Complete the full reloading sequence to fire again.

PLAYER 1 (Left Screen)

WASD - Move

Mouse - Look around

Q - Aim down sights

E - Advance reload step

F - Fire (when cocked)

PLAYER 2 (Right Screen)

Arrow Keys - Move

Numpad 4/6 or Mouse - Look

Right Shift - Aim down sights

Enter - Advance reload step

Right Ctrl - Fire (when cocked)

Reload stages: Half-cock → Bite/Pour Powder → Load Ball → Ramrod Down → Ramrod Up → Prime Pan → Full Cock → Fire

Click to lock mouse pointer
