Our Story

From a family kitchen to your table, baking with love since 1998.

Family-Owned & Passionate

Sweet Delights Bakery began in 1998 when Maria and Carlos Rodriguez turned their passion for baking into a small neighborhood shop. What started as a modest storefront with just two ovens has grown into a beloved community institution, but our commitment to quality and tradition has never changed.

Today, our children work alongside us, continuing the family tradition. We still use Maria's original sourdough starter (nicknamed "Bubbles") that she created 25 years ago, and Carlos's secret chocolate chip cookie recipe remains unchanged.
