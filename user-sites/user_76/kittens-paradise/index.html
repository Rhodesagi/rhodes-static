📚 Fun Kitten Facts

👶

Baby Teeth

Kittens are born with 26 baby teeth that fall out by 6 months, making room for 30 adult teeth!

👁️

Blue Eyes

All kittens are born with blue eyes! Their permanent eye color develops around 6-7 weeks old.

😴

Sleepyheads

Kittens sleep 16-20 hours a day because growing is exhausting work!

🍼

Purring Motor

Kittens can purr from just 2 days old! It's how they communicate with their mom.

🎯

Whisker Spots

The little dots on a kitten's face where whiskers grow are called "whisker pads."

Why Kittens? 🤔

Kittens have been delighting humans for thousands of years. These tiny balls of fur bring joy, reduce stress, and make our lives infinitely better with their antics.

Whether they're chasing invisible bugs, attacking their own tails, or falling asleep in impossibly small boxes, kittens remind us to find wonder in the simple things.
