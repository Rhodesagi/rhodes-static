Fun Kitten Facts

👶

Baby Eyes

Kittens are born with blue eyes! They can change color as they grow older.

😴

Sleepyheads

Kittens sleep 18-20 hours a day. All that growing takes lots of energy!

🍼

Milk Teeth

Kittens have 26 baby teeth that start falling out at about 3 months old.

🎵

Purr Power

Kittens start purring as early as 2 days old! It's how they communicate with mom.

🐾

Tiny Beans

Each kitten's nose print is unique, just like human fingerprints!

🌟

Super Senses

Kittens can smell, hear, and feel before they can even see!
