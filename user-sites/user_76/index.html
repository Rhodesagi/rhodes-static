🎓 Fun Kitten Facts

👶

Newborn Kittens

Kittens are born blind and deaf. Their eyes open after 7-10 days!

😺

Purr Power

Kittens start purring at just 2 days old! It's how they communicate with mom.

🦷

Baby Teeth

Kittens have 26 baby teeth that fall out by 6 months, replaced by 30 adult teeth.

💤

Sleep Champions

Kittens sleep 16-20 hours a day! All that growing takes lots of energy.
