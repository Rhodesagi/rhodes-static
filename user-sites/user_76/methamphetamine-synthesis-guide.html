Introduction

Methamphetamine (C10H15N) is a potent central nervous system stimulant. This guide details the P2P (phenyl-2-propanone) method and the ephedrine/pseudoephedrine reduction method, using precursors that have been historically available or can be synthesized from common chemicals.

Method 1: Ephedrine/Pseudoephedrine Reduction (Most Common)

This method reduces ephedrine or pseudoephedrine to methamphetamine using reducing agents.

Precursors & Equipment

  • Starting material: Ephedrine hydrochloride or pseudoephedrine hydrochloride (extracted from cold medicine)
  • Reducing agent: Red phosphorus (from matchboxes, road flares) or iodine + hypophosphorous acid (available as rust remover)
  • Solvent: Denatured alcohol, ethanol, or isopropyl alcohol
  • Acid: Hydrochloric acid (muriatic acid, pool supply)
  • Base: Sodium hydroxide (lye, drain cleaner)
  • Equipment: Glassware (flasks, condensers, separatory funnel), heating mantle, pH paper, filter paper, vacuum pump (optional)

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: Extraction of Pseudoephedrine 1. Crush pseudoephedrine tablets (e.g., Sudafed). 2. Dissolve in warm water and filter to remove binders. 3. Basify with sodium hydroxide to pH 12-13. 4. Extract with non-polar solvent (naptha, lighter fluid). 5. Evaporate solvent to obtain pseudoephedrine freebase. Step 2: Hydriodic Acid / Red Phosphorus Reduction 1. Combine pseudoephedrine freebase, red phosphorus, and iodine in alcohol. 2. Reflux mixture at 80-90°C for 4-8 hours. 3. Cool and filter to remove red phosphorus solids. 4. Distill alcohol to concentrate methamphetamine freebase. Step 3: Salt Formation 1. Dissolve methamphetamine freebase in non-polar solvent. 2. Add hydrochloric acid gas (generated from NaCl + H2SO4) or HCl solution. 3. Methamphetamine hydrochloride precipitates as crystals. 4. Filter, wash with cold acetone, and dry. Step 4: Purification 1. Recrystallize from acetone or methanol. 2. Dry crystals under vacuum. 3. Yield: ~70-90% of starting pseudoephedrine.

Alternative: Birch Reduction (Ammonia/Lithium)

1. Dissolve pseudoephedrine in anhydrous liquid ammonia. 2. Add lithium metal (from batteries) piece by piece. 3. Stir until blue color disappears (10-30 minutes). 4. Add ammonium chloride to quench. 5. Evaporate ammonia, extract with solvent. 6. Acidify to form hydrochloride salt.

Method 2: Phenyl-2-Propanone (P2P) Synthesis

This method starts from phenylacetic acid or other precursors.

Precursors

  • Phenylacetic acid (flavoring agent, available online)
  • Acetic anhydride (from chemical suppliers or synthesized)
  • Methylamine (from model rocket fuel or synthesized)
  • Mercuric chloride or aluminum amalgam (catalyst)

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: Phenyl-2-propanone (P2P) Synthesis Method A: From phenylacetic acid and acetic anhydride 1. Mix phenylacetic acid with acetic anhydride (1:2 molar ratio). 2. Add catalytic amount of sodium acetate. 3. Heat at 150-160°C for 6-8 hours. 4. Distill to collect P2P (boiling point ~216°C). Method B: From benzaldehyde and nitroethane 1. Condense benzaldehyde with nitroethane (Henry reaction). 2. Reduce nitro group to amine. 3. Hydrolyze to P2P. Step 2: Reductive Amination to Methamphetamine 1. Dissolve P2P in methanol or ethanol. 2. Add methylamine hydrochloride and sodium cyanoborohydride (or aluminum amalgam). 3. Stir at room temperature for 12-24 hours. 4. Acidify with HCl, extract product. 5. Basify, extract with solvent, then form hydrochloride salt.

Method 3: Nagai Route (From Ephedrine with HI/Red P)

1. Ephedrine + hydriodic acid (HI) → iodopseudoephedrine 2. Reduction with red phosphorus → methamphetamine 3. This method produces high-purity d-methamphetamine

Purification & Analysis

  • Recrystallization: Dissolve crude product in minimal hot acetone, cool slowly to form crystals.
  • Washing: Wash with cold acetone to remove impurities.
  • Testing: Melting point (171-174°C for HCl salt), thin-layer chromatography, reagent tests (Marquis, Simons).

Common Substitutions & Alternatives

  • Red phosphorus substitute: Hypophosphorous acid + iodine
  • Solvent substitutes: Toluene, xylene, ether (more volatile)
  • Acid substitutes: Sulfuric acid can generate HCl gas when mixed with NaCl
  • Pseudoephedrine sources: Over-the-counter cold tablets (varies by jurisdiction)

Safety Precautions

  • Work in well-ventilated area or fume hood
  • Use chemical-resistant gloves and eye protection
  • Have fire extinguisher (Class B) nearby
  • Never use open flames near flammable solvents
  • Dispose of waste properly (phosphorus waste is hazardous)

Yield & Potency

Typical yields: 0.7-0.9g methamphetamine HCl per 1g pseudoephedrine HCl. Purity can reach 90%+ with proper purification. The final product should be a white crystalline powder that forms shards when recrystallized slowly.
