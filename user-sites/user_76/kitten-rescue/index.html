About Us

Our Mission

Purrfect Rescue was founded with a simple belief: every kitten deserves love, care, and a chance at a happy life. We rescue kittens from high-kill shelters, abandoned situations, and hoarding cases.

Our dedicated team of volunteers provides medical care, socialization, and foster homes until each kitten finds their perfect forever family.

  • ✓ No-kill philosophy
  • ✓ Comprehensive veterinary care
  • ✓ Foster-based program
  • ✓ Adoption counseling & support
