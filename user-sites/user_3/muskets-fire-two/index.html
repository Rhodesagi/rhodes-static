MUSKETS FIRE — Two Player Combat

No crosshairs. Iron sights only.

  • Align rear notch with front post and target
  • Lead moving targets, account for bullet drop
  • Press R (P1) or B button (P2) to reload
  • Press T (P1) or X button (P2) to inspect lock

P1: WASD move, Mouse aim (pointer lock), Click fire
P2: Left Stick move, Right Stick aim, RT/A fire (Gamepad required)

First to 5 kills wins.
