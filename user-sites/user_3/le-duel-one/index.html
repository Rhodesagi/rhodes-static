LE DUEL

A Gentleman's Musket Duel

Player 1 (Blue Coat): WASD | Q/E Rotate | X Aim | F Fire | R Reload

Player 2 (Brown Coat): Arrows | [/] Rotate | / Aim | Enter Fire | . Reload

No crosshairs. Use iron sights. Reload carefully.

🎖️ PLAYER 1

WASD - Move

Q / E - Turn Musket

X - Raise/Lower Sights

F - Fire

R - Reload

⚔️ PLAYER 2

↑↓←→ - Move

[ / ] - Turn Musket

/ - Raise/Lower Sights

Enter - Fire

. - Reload
