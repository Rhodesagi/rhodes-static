A MUSKET DUEL IV

Two players. One keyboard. Iron sights only.

No crosshairs. No HUD. Pure skill.

HOW TO RELOAD (11 Steps):

  1. Open pan cover (tap R/P)
  2. Prime pan with powder (hold R/P)
  3. Close pan cover (tap R/P)
  4. Open frizzen (tap R/P)
  5. Pour main powder charge (hold R/P)
  6. Insert ball and patch (tap R/P)
  7. Ram cartridge home (tap R/P repeatedly)
  8. Return ramrod (hold R/P)
  9. Half-cock (tap R/P)
  10. Full-cock (tap R/P)
  11. Aim and fire (X/N to aim, F/M to fire)
