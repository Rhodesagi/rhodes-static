THE MUSKETS FIRE ONE

1776. Two men. One shot to live.

No crosshairs. Iron sights only. Reload takes 15 seconds.

PLAYER 1 (Top)

WASD - Move

MOUSE - Look/Aim

LEFT CLICK - Fire

RIGHT CLICK - Iron Sights

R - Reload

PLAYER 2 (Bottom)

ARROWS - Move

I/K - Look Up/Down

J/L - Look Left/Right

ENTER - Fire

RSHIFT - Iron Sights

RCtrl - Reload
