A Historical First-Person Flintlock Duel • Two Players • One Computer

PLAYER 1 CONTROLS

  • WASD - Move
  • Q / E - Turn Musket Left/Right
  • X - Aim Down Iron Sights
  • F - Fire
  • R - Reload (multi-step process)

PLAYER 2 CONTROLS

  • Arrow Keys - Move
  • N / M - Turn Musket Left/Right
  • K - Aim Down Iron Sights
  • J - Fire
  • L - Reload

GAME RULES

• First to 3 hits wins the duel

• No crosshairs - use iron sights only

• Full historical reload process required after each shot

• Movement limited to small dueling ground

RELOAD PROCESS (Historical Flintlock)

  1. 1. Half-cock the musket (safety)
  2. 2. Pour powder from powder horn
  3. 3. Ram ball and patch down barrel
  4. 4. Prime the pan with fine powder
  5. 5. Full-cock and ready to fire

Press R (Player 1) or L (Player 2) to begin reload, then follow on-screen prompts.
