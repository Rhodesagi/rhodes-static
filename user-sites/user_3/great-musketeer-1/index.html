Le Musket Quatre - Two Player Musket Duel
Player 1: 100 HP
Ammo: 0
Status: Ready
Player 2: 100 HP
Ammo: 0
Status: Ready

Controls

Player 1: WASD - Move, Q/E - Tilt Musket Left/Right, R - Reload, F - Fire, X - Aim
Player 2: Arrow Keys - Move, Numpad 4/6 - Tilt Musket, Numpad 0 - Reload, Numpad . - Fire, Numpad 5 - Aim

Reloading Process

1. Pour Powder
2. Ram Ball
3. Prime Pan
4. Cock Hammer
5. Ready to Fire
